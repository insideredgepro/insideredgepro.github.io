Why the Market Watches Pelosi's Trades

Nancy Pelosi is one of the most closely watched traders in Congress. Under the STOCK Act (Stop Trading on Congressional Knowledge Act), every member of Congress must disclose personal and spousal stock trades within 45 days of the transaction. InsiderEdgePro surfaces these disclosures the moment they hit the public record — so you never miss a filing.
